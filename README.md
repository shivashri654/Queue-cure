📌 Overview

The Smart Hospital Queue & Consultation Management System is designed to modernize hospital workflows by replacing traditional token systems with an intelligent, real-time digital platform.

The system enables seamless coordination between receptionists, doctors, patients, and administrators while reducing waiting times and improving patient experience through live updates, automated notifications, and digital consultation management.
